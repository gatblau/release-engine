# Prompt for Generating the Release Engine Logo

---

**Design a minimal, modern logo icon for an open-source project called "Release Engine."**

**Concept:** The Release Engine is an idempotent job scheduler — the central execution backbone of a DevOps/AIOps platform. Every action flows through it before reaching infrastructure. Think of it as a turbine or engine core that all operational intent must pass through: durable, observable, concurrency-safe, and cryptographically secure.

**Visual direction:**

- A stylised **engine turbine or rotor** viewed head-on, with clean geometric blades radiating from a central hub — evoking power, rotation, and orchestration
- Integrate subtle **flow arrows or pipeline channels** converging into the center, representing all intent (human, AI, automated) funneling through a single execution core
- Inside or behind the turbine, hint at a **lock or shield motif** (a small keyhole, a segmented vault ring, or concentric encrypted layers) to represent the embedded Volta encryption and multi-tenant cryptographic isolation
- Optionally incorporate a **play/deploy triangle** or a **gear tooth edge** on the outer ring to reinforce the "release" and "engine" concepts

**Style constraints:**

- Flat, vector-based, monochrome-friendly (should work in single color on dark and light backgrounds)
- Geometric and symmetric — no gradients, no photorealism
- Suitable as a GitHub repository avatar (legible at 64×64 px, striking at 512×512 px)
- Aesthetic family: industrial precision meets developer tooling — think Kubernetes wheel, Terraform hexagon, or Argo CD circle, but distinctly its own
- Color palette suggestion: deep steel blue (#1B2A4A) as primary, with an accent of electric teal (#00D4AA) or warm amber (#F59E0B) for energy; but design primarily for monochrome first

**Do NOT include:**

- Any text or wordmarks (icon only)
- Overly complex detail that collapses at small sizes
- Clichéd rocket ships, clouds, or generic gear icons without the turbine/flow concept

**Deliverable:** A single square icon, centered on a transparent background, with bold silhouette clarity.
